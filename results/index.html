What Your Result Means

Your result is an educational match based on the choices you made in the checker. It does not approve you for Lifeline service, a free phone, a free iPhone, or a tablet. Final decisions depend on the National Verifier, state rules, provider inventory, household rules, and required documents.

If you selected SNAP, EBT, Medicaid, SSI, veterans pension, Federal Public Housing Assistance, or an income-based path, your next step is to compare provider pages and prepare documents before visiting an official application page.

Recommended Next Steps

  1. Read your document checklist. Confirm ID, benefit proof, income proof, and address details before you apply.
  2. Compare provider availability. Some providers are better for basic phone service, while others may advertise special device offers.
  3. Check iPhone and tablet expectations. iPhone and tablet availability is more limited than basic Android phone availability.
  4. Open the provider page. Use the apply section only after you understand terms, eligibility, and possible device limits.
