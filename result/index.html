FAQ

Does this result mean I am approved?

No. The result is based only on your selections. Final approval is handled by a provider, the National Verifier, or a state process where applicable.

Why does the page show multiple provider cards?

Provider fit depends on state availability, eligibility route, device inventory, and plan rules. Compare before applying.

Should I prepare documents first?

Yes. A clear benefit letter, Medicaid or SNAP proof, identity document, and address proof can reduce delays if manual verification is needed.
