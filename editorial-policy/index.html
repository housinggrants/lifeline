Editorial Policy

Our editorial goal is to help visitors understand Lifeline and provider options without false guarantees. We write for people who may be searching for free government phone, EBT phone, Medicaid phone, free iPhone, or free tablet options, while keeping the difference between eligibility and device availability clear.

Advertisement

We use official Lifeline, FCC, USAC, provider, and consumer-safety sources where relevant. We avoid hidden keywords, fake government claims, fake urgency, and guaranteed device wording. We do not place ads inside checker questions or directly beside answer buttons.

Provider pages are informational and may include external provider links near the end. If a provider relationship or monetized link is used, the link is marked with appropriate rel attributes. Editorial warnings remain independent from ad placement.
