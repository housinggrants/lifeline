Editorial Policy

Read how we research, review and update Lifeline, free phone, EBT phone, provider and document information for consumers.

Research standards

We write Lifeline, free phone, EBT phone, Medicaid phone, tablet, and provider content with cautious language. We do not promise guaranteed approval, guaranteed free iPhones, or universal device availability. We distinguish between Lifeline service eligibility, provider offers, device inventory, and final verification.

Update process

Provider pages and eligibility pages are reviewed for wording that may become outdated. When an offer, application screen, or policy detail may change, we use availability language and encourage users to verify current details with official resources or the provider before applying.

Corrections

If a page contains outdated wording, unclear warnings, or a broken link, we review the correction and update the page when the change improves consumer clarity.
