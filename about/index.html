About Lifeline Options Assistant

Learn how this independent Lifeline phone options assistant helps consumers compare eligibility paths, documents and provider choices.

Independent consumer assistance, not a government agency

Lifeline Options Assistant is an independent consumer information site connected to Housing Grants Finder. The site helps visitors organize Lifeline eligibility questions, free phone options, EBT phone paths, Medicaid phone paths, free iPhone availability notes, free tablet availability notes, documents, and provider comparisons.

We do not decide eligibility, enroll users, collect SSN, collect phone numbers, or collect full addresses through the checker. Final approval is handled by a provider, the National Verifier, or an applicable state process. Our goal is to help users move through a safer application path before they click external provider pages.

How this site helps

  • Shows a no-private-data eligibility assistant.
  • Explains provider availability and device availability warnings.
  • Organizes document checklists and application mistakes.
  • Links to official resources near the end of research pages.
