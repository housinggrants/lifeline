Privacy Policy

This site is designed to avoid collecting sensitive personal data through the eligibility checker. The checker does not ask for name, email, phone number, Social Security number, full address, date of birth, benefit document, or income document.

Advertisement

The checker may use browser storage or session storage to remember your selections for the result page. This is used only to display your result in your browser. Static pages are hosted without a custom user account system.

Advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies according to their own policies. Advertisement areas are labeled. External provider or official links may take you to websites with different privacy practices. Review those policies before entering personal information.
