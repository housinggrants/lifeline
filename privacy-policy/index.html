Privacy approach

The eligibility checker uses buttons and a state dropdown. It does not ask for name, email, phone number, SSN, benefit ID, full address, or uploaded documents. Selections may be stored in browser session storage only to display the result page.

Advertising and cookies

This site uses Ad.plus advertising. Advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, and related technologies according to their own policies. Ad spaces are labeled as Advertisement and are reserved to reduce layout shift.

Do not send private documents

Do not send benefit documents, identity documents, SSN, or full application details through this independent site.
