Avoid Free Phone Scams: Red Flags Before You Apply

Learn warning signs around fake free phone, free iPhone, free tablet, EBT, and government benefit offers.

Advertisement

Scam pages often promise guaranteed iPhones, ask for urgent payments or blur the line between government programs and private offers.

Be careful with pages that use government seals without permission, hide ownership or ask for sensitive documents too early.

Use official provider pages and recognized verification systems when submitting private information.

Best next steps

  • Check whether your route is program-based or income-based.
  • Compare provider availability, documents and device terms.
  • Keep external application steps until after you understand the terms.
