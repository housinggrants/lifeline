FAQ

Is this website the official Lifeline application?

No. This is an independent guide connected to Housing Grants Finder. Final eligibility and approval are handled by a provider, the National Verifier, or a state process where applicable.

Do I need to enter my SSN here?

No. The checker on this site does not ask for SSN, email, phone number, name, or full address. It only helps you organize your next step.

Can I get a guaranteed free iPhone?

No independent guide can guarantee a free iPhone. Some providers may offer smartphones or upgraded devices depending on state, eligibility, inventory, and current offer terms.

Does EBT or SNAP help with Lifeline eligibility?

SNAP is one of the common program paths used for Lifeline eligibility. You may still need to verify your details through the National Verifier or the provider application process.
