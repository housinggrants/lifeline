Ad.plus full-site revamp summary, 2026-05-23

This build is optimized for stronger ad visibility, safer UX, better internal link flow, and fresher site design.

Ad stack:
- Interstitial remains active on normal HTML pages.
- Bottom anchor remains active.
- Desktop side rails remain active on large screens.
- In-content display slots and auto injected slots remain active.
- InPage Video remains active, one video unit per page where a video reserve exists.
- No-fill display ad wrappers now collapse to avoid empty ad boxes.
- GPT is loaded once through app.js.
- Refresh remains limited to out-of-page sticky and side rail units.

SEO and UX:
- Added /free-samsung-phone/ for the high-intent Samsung Android device path.
- Added high-intent cards on the homepage.
- Added a keyword-led intent table on /compare-options/.
- Strengthened footer and internal links to all money pages.
- Added official verification source links where pages lacked external trust links.
- Updated stylesheet for a cleaner 2026 design refresh.

Safety notes:
- No page promises a guaranteed iPhone, Samsung phone, tablet, or approval.
- Device wording stays conditional on eligibility, state, provider, inventory, and terms.
- Official source boxes point users to FCC, LifelineSupport.org, USAC National Verifier, and the USAC ACP notice.
