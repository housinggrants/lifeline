Quick answer

What should you compare in a Lifeline phone plan?

Do not compare only the device headline. Compare monthly cost, talk and text terms, data amount, hotspot rules, network coverage, number transfer, replacement fees, support, recertification reminders, and device availability in your state.

The best plan path is the one that matches your eligibility, state, usage, documents, and provider coverage, not only the page that says free phone.

FAQ

Are all Lifeline phone plans the same?

No. Plan terms, coverage, data, support, device wording, and state availability vary by provider.

Should I choose a provider only because it says free phone?

No. Check eligibility, plan terms, coverage, device condition, possible fees, and documents before applying.

Can I transfer my number to a Lifeline provider?

Some providers may support number transfer, but terms vary. Check the provider's own transfer instructions before applying.
